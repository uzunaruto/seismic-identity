03 What this is, what it isn't

Is

  • A free identity primitive for early Seismic users
  • A testnet proof of your presence on chain
  • A permanent record of how you signed your name
  • Open source, no backend, no central registry

Isn't

  • Not a token, not an NFT you can flip
  • Not connected to a roadmap or fundraising
  • Not a profile picture generator for X
  • Not affiliated with the Seismic Foundation
On the record

Seismic Signatures is built and run by community contributors. The contract address is published on the collection page once it is deployed to testnet. Until then, minting stores the artifact locally in your browser.
